About The Chatbot

We would like you to describe your usual diet with the chatbot, give short and clear answer based on the chatbot's questions. The chatbot could help you complete the questionnaires more convenient and faster.

About The Forms

We would like you to describe your usual diet over the last 2-3 months. This should include all your main meals, snacks and drinks which you had at home or away from home e.g. at work, at restaurants or cafes and with friends and family. For Every line in the questionnaire, we would like you to answer two things.

  • how much of the food you had in a day you ate the food, and
  • how many days a week you had the food.
